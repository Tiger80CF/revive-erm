Susan Ramonat

15 years building ERM programs across asset management, investment processing, fund administration, health insurance, and life insurance. The common thread: organizations where the basics weren't working and the emerging risks weren't visible.

Over nearly 20 years at SEI Investments, Susan served in a variety of roles including Head of Enterprise Risk Management. She has held senior risk positions at Vanguard and WPS Health Solutions, and served on the Investment Company Institute's CRO Committee.

Known for her foresight and pragmatism, Susan speaks frequently at industry conferences and universities about risk management, emerging technology, and the intersection of strategic and operational risk.
